Transfer and (Anything)

From columns (or other applicable formation): The #1 and #2 dancers Transfer The Column, omitting the final Extend, ending as an in-facing couple on the outside. The other dancers walk forward to form a box (or other compact formation; this may be less than a full circulate) in the center and do the (Anything) call.

Example: Transfer and Quarter Thru

Example: Transfer and Crossfire

Example: Transfer and Cut the Diamond

Example: Transfer and Peel and Trail

Timing: 8 (any starts on 5)
