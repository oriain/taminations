Grand follow your neighbor

From Columns: The #1 dancer in each column does the leads' part of follow your neighbor ("Fold and Roll"). The #2,3, and 4 dancers do the trailers' part of Follow Your Neighbor (Extend and Cast Off ¾). Ends in a tidal wave.


You can also do Grand Follow Your Neighbor and Spread.

Timing: 6
