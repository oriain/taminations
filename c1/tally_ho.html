Tally Ho

From parallel waves or lines, trade-by, and 2x4 T-Bones: All ½ Circulate. The centers Hinge and ½ Box Circulate, while the outsides Trade. Those who meet Cast ¾ while the other four Hourglass Circulate, to form parallel waves or lines, or a parallelogram.
