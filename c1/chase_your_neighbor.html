Chase Your Neighbor

From couples back to back: Start a Chase Right, blending into a Follow Your Neighbor: The original beaus begin the Chase Right, meet each other, and Cast Off ¾. The original belles begin the Zoom action, but after passing each other, they "Fold and Roll" to finish as the ends of a wave.
